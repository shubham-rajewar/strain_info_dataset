# strain_info_dataset
The strain tensors generated using more than 1600 GNSS velocities in Indian reference frame of and around the Indian Plate.
#Header infromation for strain_info
#columns:
1  Latitude (grid)
2  Longitude (grid)
3  vx (East velocity)
4  dvx (sigma error)
5  vy (North velocity)
6  dvx (sigma error)
7  w (rotation)
8  dw (sigma error)
9  exx (strain tensor)
10 dexx (sigma error)
11 exy (strain tensor)
12 dexy (sigma error)
13 eyy (strain tensor)
14 deyy (sigma error)
15 emax (principal axes)
16 demax (sigma error)
17 emin (principal axes)
18 demin (sigma error)
19 shr (maximum shear)
20 dshr (sigma error)
21 azi (azimuth)
22 dazi (sigma error)
23 dilat (dilatational strain)
24 ddilat (sigma error)
25 sec. invariant (second Invariant strain)
26 dsecinv (sigma error)
